# JTBD Framework Recap

From "When Coffee and Kale Compete", the key principles:

1. **People don't buy products, they hire them to make progress**
2. **Four Forces of Progress shape demand**:
   - **Push** - Problems that force people to seek change
   - **Pull** - Vision of a better life
   - **Anxiety** - Fear of the new solution
   - **Inertia/Habits** - Comfort with current way

3. **Competition is broader than you think** - Any solution that helps make progress competes
4. **Zero-sum game** - When someone uses your solution, they stop using something else
5. **Progress over outcomes** - Design for ongoing feeling of progress
