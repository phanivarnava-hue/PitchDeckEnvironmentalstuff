### What is CSRD?

**Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive** - EU regulation requiring companies to publicly report detailed sustainability data across environmental, social, and governance (ESG) topics.

**Key Requirements:**
- "Double materiality" assessment - Report both (1) how sustainability affects business AND (2) company's impact on sustainability
- Covers: emissions, water use, biodiversity, labor practices, human rights, corruption, board diversity, etc.
- Must be audited and published in annual report
- Standardized format (European Sustainability Reporting Standards - ESRS)

**Who Must Comply:**
- **Large EU companies:** >1,000 employees AND >‚Ç¨450M turnover (updated Dec 2025)
- **Non-EU companies:** >‚Ç¨450M EU turnover + EU subsidiary >‚Ç¨200M turnover
- **Phased timeline:**
  - 2025 FY (report 2026): Already-listed large companies
  - 2026 FY (report 2027): Other large companies ‚Üê **BIGGEST WAVE**
  - 2027 FY (report 2028): Listed SMEs
